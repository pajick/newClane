CLANE

History notes on an Irish village

The village of Clane, Co. Kildare is situated in the northern part of the county. Some thirty-four kilometers west of the capital city of Dublin, and thirteen kilometers from the university town of Maynooth. Clane is not as well known nationally, perhaps, as some small villages, it has however played a long and interesting part in the history of Ireland...
